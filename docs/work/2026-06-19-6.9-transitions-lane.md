# Scene Transitions ‚Äî Slice A: framework + Lane Drop / Lane Launch

**Date:** 2026-06-19
**Branch:** feature/transitions-lane
**Roadmap item:** Phase 6 ‚Äî 6.9 Scene transitions (slice 1 of 2)

## Goal

A reusable transition driver, plus the reversible **Lane Drop** (hyperspace‚Üíorbit)
and **Lane Launch** (orbit/node‚Üíhyperspace) animations, so arriving at and
departing nodes reads as travel rather than a hard cut. (Drop Pod Deploy is Slice B.)

Already landed on this branch as the first commit: the **orbit-ship fix** (orbit
now shows the real hull + components).

## Approach

**Transition driver (new `src/renderer/transition.ts`).** A full-screen overlay
container added on top of the stage that plays a timed timeline off the app ticker
and resolves a callback/Promise on completion. main.ts plays it *at a phase seam*,
swapping the phase when it completes. Honors reduced motion (collapses to a ~0.2s
flash-cut). FX-beat law: the scene is Resurrect 64 (reuse the lane coral backdrop
art); only the cyan drop/jump flash + the orange/cyan engine wash fire as pure
accents, on their beat.

**Lane Drop** (~2.4s, at arrival‚Üíorbit): the lane coral tunnel decelerates
(ease-out on scroll speed) ‚Üí cyan drop-flash ‚Üí tunnel collapses away as the planet
swings into the lower third ‚Üí hand off to the orbit screen.

**Lane Launch** (~2.5s, at depart‚Üílane): the *Drop reversed* ‚Äî brief calm, an
orange+cyan engine-wash spool-up (~0.6s), cyan jump-flash, the coral funnel blooms
inward and accelerates ‚Üí hand off to the lane/combat. Built as one timeline played
forward (launch) / reversed (drop) per the design doc, sharing the coral + flash
assets.

**Seams in `main.ts`:**
- Lane Drop: in the planet-arrival path (`arriveAtNode` ‚Üí `enterOrbit`) ‚Äî play Drop,
  then `enterOrbit` on complete.
- Lane Launch: in the departure path (`travelToNode` ‚Üí `startLane`) ‚Äî play Launch,
  then `startLane` on complete.

Assets are existing: lane coral backdrop (`laneBackdrop`), a cyan quad, an additive
glow quad for the engine wash, and the planet sprite (`createAnimatedPlanet`/Deep-Fold).
No new hand-drawn art (per the design doc).

## What actually happened

Built as planned. Decisions/notes:

- **Orbit-ship fix** (first commit): added `compositeShipFromModules(hullId, names)`
  to `sprites.ts` (the shared slot-mapping combat uses, deduplicated out of
  `space-renderer.ts`), threaded `hullId` through `orbit-mode`/`main`. Orbit now
  shows the real hull. **Browser-verified** (Freighter + Scout render their real
  silhouettes in orbit, not the default Gunship).
- **Transition driver** (`renderer/transition.ts`): a self-contained overlay that
  renders a radial warp-streak field (muted R64 coral) with a cyan punch flash and
  an orange/cyan engine-wash glow, off the app ticker. `lane-drop` runs the speed
  curve decelerating (rush‚Üístop‚Üíflash‚Üícollapse); `lane-launch` runs it accelerating
  (calm‚Üíspool-up wash‚Üíflash‚Üífunnel out). Reduced-motion collapses to a ~0.22s cyan
  flash. `cancel()` lets `main.ts` kill it on teardown.
- **New `'transition'` GamePhase**: shows the canvas only (no DOM overlay matches
  it); added to the page.tsx world-framing condition. The Pixi canvas is always
  mounted (page.tsx:180), so the overlay survives the phase.
- **Seams** (`main.ts`): `runTransition(kind, onComplete)` sets the phase and plays
  the overlay. Lane Drop wraps the planet branch of `arriveAtNode` ‚Üí `enterOrbit`;
  Lane Launch wraps `selectNode` ‚Üí `startLane`. `activeTransition` is cancelled in
  the handle `destroy()`.

**Browser verification (preview, foreground-limited):** Lane Launch triggers on
node departure and renders correctly ‚Äî warp streaks accelerating outward + engine
wash, with the DOM hidden (canvas-only). Confirmed the animation advances over
time. The headless preview throttles rAF between screenshots, so the timed
completion (‚Üí `startLane`) and the cyan flash beat couldn't be captured headlessly,
and **Lane Drop** needs a planet arrival (a full combat playthrough) ‚Äî both want a
**human foreground playthrough** for final feel sign-off. 529 tests green;
lint/type-check clean.

## Rework (playtest feedback, same session)

First pass was an abstract centred radial burst ‚Äî wrong. Reworked to the design:
the **ship stays in frame** (composited hull + components, screen-centre), wrapped
in a **pixelated coral spiral** (chunky `CHUNK`-snapped squares) that **closes in**
on the Drop and **blooms outward** on the Launch, with a **camera push** (world
scale ease) for smoothness. The **Drop** rises the planet from below to the orbit
pose and eases the ship to the orbit-screen position so `enterOrbit` hands off
seamlessly behind the cyan flash; the **Launch** spools the orange/cyan engine
wash, flashes, then accelerates the spiral out. Added a **`?transition=` dev knob**
(loops a transition in isolation) so the feel can be tuned without a lane run.
Browser-verified the new composition for both: ship in frame, pixelated spiral,
camera push, correct hull (Gunship/Scout), Launch engine wash. Planet-rise +
seamless settle + overall smoothness still want a human foreground pass (the
headless preview throttles rAF so those phases/timing can't be captured here).
